Real-world analogy

Old T9 keypad: 2 = a/b/c, 3 = d/e/f. Each digit is a wheel of letters. Fix one letter on wheel 1, spin through all of wheel 2, then come back and change wheel 1. Every root-to-leaf path through this tree is one combination.
