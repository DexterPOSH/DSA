K Closest Points to Origin — Max-Heap of Size k

Real-world analogy

Ek delivery dispatcher ko 2 sabse paas wale riders chahiye. Board pe sirf 2 "current closest" rakhe jaate hain, aur sabse door wala top pe dikhta hai. Naya rider closer hua to door wale ko hatao. Wo board ek max-heap of size k hai — root = farthest, evict karne ke liye ready.

points = [[1,3], [-2,2], [5,8], [0,1]], k = 2. Rank by dist² = x² + y² (no sqrt needed). Keep a max-heap of the k smallest.

Key takeaway

Keep a max-heap of size k ordered by dist². The root is the farthest kept point, so a closer newcomer instantly evicts it. Result: the k closest in O(n log k) — and never compute a sqrt.
