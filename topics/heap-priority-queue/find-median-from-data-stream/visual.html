Find Median from Data Stream — two heaps for streaming median

Azure Stream Analytics-style median state: lower half max-heap, upper half min-heap, median at the boundary.

📊 Analogy: Azure Stream Analytics running median

An Azure Stream Analytics job can track a live median metric by splitting values into two priority queues. The low half uses a max-heap, the high half uses a min-heap, and rebalancing keeps the median at the boundary.

Key takeaway

Like Azure Stream Analytics median state, keep the lower half in a max-heap and the upper half in a min-heap, balanced to within one element — the median stays available in O(1).
